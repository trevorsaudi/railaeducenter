Welcome to Raila Educational Center

Raila educational center is a mixed day and boarding school in Nairobi serving the local community at primary and secondary school levels. We foster courage, generosity and resolve so that our children emerge empowered with the skills and abilities to be the leaders and guardians of their communities.

Our school values

The school believes students learn best when they feel safe, valued and happy. Our staff is committed to providing an environment that nurtures personal growth and self-esteem.

Cooperation

'Sharing and learning together'. Cooperation is working together in a team, contributing and sharing in a helpful way.

Achievement

'Persist and try your best'. Achievement is striving to attain your personal goals, persisting and giving your best effort.

empathy

'Treat others with care and compassion'. Empathy means trying to understand other people's views or feelings and being supportive and caring towards them.
